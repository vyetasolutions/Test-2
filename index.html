About SHELENG

Who We Are

Headquartered in Lusaka, Zambia, SHELENG is a premier logistics and trading house specialized in bridging African mineral wealth with international demand.

Mission

To engineer seamless supply chains and transparent trading platforms that maximize value for mining producers, buyers, and regional economies.

Vision

To be the undisputed leader in African resource logistics, setting global standards for operational excellence, safety, and sustainable trade.
